What is sentiment analysis

Sentiment analysis is to extract the information from an individual about attitute toward the current sitution. The sentiment analysis in the socical media is to analyze based on the text posted by an individual, and then the coresponding score for the sentiment analysis will be produced to see what their attitude is such as positive, negative and neutral.

Factor Selection

The factors we choose are obesity rate, education (graduation rate of bachelor), smoking rate, alcohol abuse rate and average income in each corresponding area where twitter users post their tweets. Firstly, the obesity and alcohol abuse can represent the diet and exercise of people in the corresponding area, and how the diet and exercise influence emotion. Secondly, the education data could represent whether the people accepted high level of education are happier than those who doesn't accept. Thirdly, the income could represent whether high income could bring more happiness or not. At last, we want to explore the relationship between sentiment and smoking rate. All data mentioned above are from AURIN.
